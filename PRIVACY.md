# Privacy Policy

**StreamClient**

*Last updated: March 23, 2026*

## Overview

StreamClient is a local network streaming client that connects to your personal PVR/DVR server. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

StreamClient does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app:

- Does not collect analytics or usage data
- Does not collect crash reports
- Does not use advertising or tracking frameworks
- Does not collect device identifiers
- Does not share data with third parties

## Network Communication

StreamClient communicates exclusively with the PVR/DVR server you configure (NextPVR or Dispatcharr). All network traffic stays between your device and your server on your local network or via the address you provide. No data is sent to the developer or any third-party service.

## iCloud Sync

StreamClient uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store to sync your preferences (server address, topic keywords, and playback settings) across your own Apple devices. This data is managed entirely by Apple under your iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer.

## Local Storage

All app data — including server credentials, playback positions, and preferences — is stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud account. Nothing is stored on external servers controlled by the developer.

## Children's Privacy

StreamClient does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.
